cooking


This is a picture of chocolate covered Macaroons. I know that in the picture there are only ten. The batch made out around 20 Macaroons, and my family and I ate the rest of them. They were amazing! Here is the recipe:

Ingredients
  • 2 1/2 cups dried unsweetened shredded coconut
  • 1 1/2 tbsp potato starch
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 4 large egg whites
  • 1 tsp vanilla (optional)
  • 13 oz dark chocolate, divided
😳
step1) In a large mixing bowl, use fork to stir together the shredded coconut, potato starch, sugar and salt till well blended. Break up any chunks of potato starch.

step2) In a smaller bowl, whisk together the egg whites and vanilla till frothy (bubbles covering the mixture)

step3) Pour egg white mixture into dried coconut mixture. Stir batter together, making sure the coconut is fully and evenly moistened by egg whites. Stir for 5 minutes. Let mixture sit for 20-30 minutes while the coconut rehydrate's.

step4) Preheat oven to 325 degrees F. Stir batter again. Line baking sheet with parchment paper or with a Silpat. Start forming the macaroons. They won't spread, so you can bake them close together.

step5) The batter will be delicate, it won't "hold" together the way cookie dough does, as they bake they'll stick together. When you've dropped all of the batter onto your baking sheets, use wet fingers to gently shape the macaroons into rough domes or haystacks. Don't worry, they're not supposed to be perfect.

step6) Bake the macaroons for 20-25 minutes till the bottom edges turn golden and the tips of the coconut shreds start to brown. Remove from the oven. Don't over-bake or they will become dry. If by the end of the 25 minutes the macaroons are not solid, then continue to bake them for another 15 minutes, but make sure that they dont OVER COOK.

step7) Let the macaroons cool, before moving them. Trying to move them too soon will cause them to crumble.
step8) When the macaroons have cooled completely, you can dip and decorate them to your wish. If wanted use a piping bag, if you dont have one just use a zip loc bag and cut a small hole on one corner.
